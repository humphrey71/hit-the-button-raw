Explore Hit The Button Game Modes

Number Bonds

Practice making number pairs that add up to target numbers. Hit The Button offers number bonds to 10, 20, and 100, helping build a strong foundation in addition and mental arithmetic.

Times Tables

Master multiplication facts from 2 to 12 times tables. Choose between multiplication and division questions, with options for mixed tables practice to challenge yourself further.

Doubling & Halving

Strengthen mental calculation skills with doubling numbers up to 100 and halving both even and odd numbers. Perfect for building number sense and understanding relationships between numbers.

Square Numbers

Practice identifying square numbers and their roots. Hit The Button makes learning these important mathematical concepts engaging and memorable.

Division Facts

Build confidence with division through targeted practice of division facts up to 12. Progress from basic facts to more challenging mixed divisions.

Multiples

Explore number patterns and relationships by practicing multiples of numbers from 2 to 12. Great for developing multiplication fluency and number pattern recognition.

Timed Challenges

Test your speed and accuracy with 60-second challenges in any game mode. Try to beat your high score and track your improvement over time with Hit The Button's scoring system.

Practice Mode

Learn at your own pace with untimed practice sessions. Perfect for building confidence and mastering new concepts before taking on the timed challenges.
